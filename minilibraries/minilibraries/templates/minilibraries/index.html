How it works:

  1. Join a library

    At present, there's only one library available (this is only a prototype)

  2. Register your books

    You can register your copy of a book by using it's ISBN (usually found on the back of the book)

  3. Borrow and share

    You now have access to the collection of everyone else in your libraries. You can request any book you find on the website, and organise transfer with the owner. Once you're done, return the book to it's owner and mark the book as being returned.
